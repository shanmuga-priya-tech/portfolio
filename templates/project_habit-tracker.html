Welcome to my HabitTracker app, a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you seamlessly integrate positive habits into your daily routine. With this app, you can create and track as many habits as you desire, effortlessly marking them as complete and observing your progress over time.The user-friendly interface ensures a smooth experience as you manage your habits with ease.

Features:

  • Custom Habit Creation: Tailor your habit list according to your preferences by creating new habits that align with your goals and aspirations.
  • Effortless Completion Tracking: Keep tabs on your progress by effortlessly marking habits as complete, allowing you to visually gauge your consistency.
  • Seamless Deletion: If your goals evolve or circumstances change, you have the flexibility to easily delete habits at any time.
  • Automated Integration: Once a habit is created, it becomes a recurring part of your daily routine. No need to add it repeatedly; the app takes care of that for you.

  • HTML
  • CSS
